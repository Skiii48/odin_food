Steak Tartare

Quick, Easy & Simple

Ingredients

  • Beef (Sirloin) - 250g
  • shallots - 1 small
  • Capers - 30g
  • Girkin - 30g
  • Egg yolk - 1
  • Tabasco - To taste
  • Woerstershire Sauce - To taste

Optional

  • Dijon mustard - 2/4 Teaspoon
  • Fresh coriander
  • chilli flakes - 1/4 Teaspoon

Method

  1. Cut the beef very thinly using a very sharp knife
  2. Slice the shallots, cappers & girkins
  3. Mix all cute Ingredients together
  4. place the yolk on top
