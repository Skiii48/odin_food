Bone Marrow

Our hot take on this classic dish

One of our all time favorites, having become increasingly popular over the past few years. Here is our winning recipe for one of the all time classic dishes

What you'll need

  • Bone marrow canoes - 500g
  • Anchovies
  • Garlic
  • Salt & pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Parsley leaves
    •

    Method

    1. Preheat the oven
    2. Lay the marrow on a oven proof tray and bake for 10mins
    3. In a food processor, add all the ingredients and blitz till a fine paste
    4. After the 10minutes has passed, remove the bone marrow from the oven. Spoon on the crumb, so that it covers the whole bone, add a touch more seasoning and cook for another 10minutes. Or until golden and crisp.
